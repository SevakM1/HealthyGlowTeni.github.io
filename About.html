Meet the esthetician: Teni Keshishyan

Hello, I'm Teni Keshishyan, an experienced esthetician dedicated to providing exceptional skincare services. With a passion for helping individuals achieve healthy, radiant skin, I'm thrilled to be a part of your skincare journey.
With years of industry experience, I have honed my skills and developed a deep understanding of skincare. I specialize in addressing various skin concerns, such as acne, aging, hyperpigmentation, and more. By staying updated with the latest research and industry trends, I ensure that my clients receive the most effective treatments and personalized skincare solutions.
My approach to skincare is rooted in science and customization. I believe that each person's skin is unique, and I take pride in tailoring treatments to suit individual needs. By carefully assessing your skin and listening to your concerns, I create customized regimens and provide expert advice to help you achieve your desired results.
During our sessions, I create a serene and welcoming environment where you can relax and unwind. I believe that skincare is not just about external beauty but also about fostering inner confidence and well-being. I aim to provide a rejuvenating experience that leaves you feeling refreshed and revitalized.
I am committed to using high-quality, dermatologist-approved products in my treatments. I carefully select products that are free from harsh chemicals and cruelty-free, ensuring that your skin receives the best care without compromising your health.
Beyond our sessions, I am dedicated to empowering you with knowledge and guidance. I will provide you with practical tips and recommendations for maintaining your skin's health and beauty at home. Your skincare journey doesn't end when you leave my spa; I am here to support you every step of the way.
Throughout my career, I have dedicated myself to expanding my knowledge and skill set. I have pursued advanced training in cutting-edge skincare techniques, attended industry conferences, and stayed abreast of the latest scientific developments. This continuous learning allows me to offer innovative treatments and stay ahead of the curve, ensuring that you receive the best solutions for your unique skin concerns.
When you step into my treatment room, I want you to feel embraced by a tranquil atmosphere. I believe that skincare is not just a routine, but an opportunity to indulge in self-care and relaxation. From the moment you enter, I will create a serene space where you can leave your worries behind and focus on rejuvenating your mind, body, and spirit.
I understand that skincare is a personal journey, and I am here to guide you every step of the way. I will take the time to truly understand your skin's needs, your lifestyle, and your goals. By building a strong foundation of trust and communication, we will work together to develop a comprehensive skincare plan that aligns with your unique preferences and aspirations.
Using only the finest skincare products, I am committed to providing you with treatments that not only deliver results but also nourish and protect your skin. I have carefully curated a selection of top-quality products that are not only effective but are also ethically sourced and environmentally friendly. Your well-being and the health of our planet are of utmost importance to me.
Beyond the treatment room, I am dedicated to empowering you with the knowledge and tools to maintain your skin's health in your daily life. I will educate you on the importance of a consistent skincare routine, offer personalized recommendations for homecare products, and share practical tips for preserving your skin's youthful vitality. Together, we will ensure that your skin remains luminous and vibrant long after your visit.
I am sincerely honored to be a part of your skincare journey. It is my greatest joy to witness the positive impact that radiant, healthy skin can have on your confidence and overall well-being. Book an appointment with me today, and let us embark on this exciting and transformative path to beautiful skin together.
